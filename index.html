About

Light Luck Quest

At Light Luck Quest, we believe in the power of mindfulness, self-reflection, and holistic well-being. Our retreat is a, sanctuary designed to help individuals disconnect from stress, refocus their minds, and embark on a journey of inner discovery.

Nestled in the peaceful surroundings of Konya, our retreat center offers a blend of guided meditation, yoga, nature therapy, and transformative workshops to help you cultivate clarity, resilience, and personal fulfillment.

Our

Retreat Programs

Mindfulness & Meditation Retreat

A rejuvenating experience that introduces meditation techniques, breathing exercises, and guided visualization to promote mental clarity and emotional balance.

Yoga & Body Awareness Retreat

Designed for those looking to harmonize mind and body through mindful movement, stretching, and deep relaxation practices.

Nature Healing & Digital Detox

Step away from technology and reconnect with nature. This program includes forest bathing, hiking, and mindful outdoor activities to rejuvenate the soul.

Personal Growth & Self-Discovery Workshop

A deep exploration into self-awareness, personal aspirations, and goal-setting. Perfect for those seeking direction, purpose, and emotional resilience.
